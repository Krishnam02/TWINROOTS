Meeting Gulf Market Requirements

Gulf countries have specific standards for imported onions, including:

  • Size and Uniformity: Sorted by size and weight for retail and industrial buyers.
  • Low Moisture Content: Properly cured onions (under 6% moisture) prevent spoilage in transit.
  • Appearance: Clean, firm, blemish-free, and sprout-free onions only.
  • Packaging: Export-ready mesh bags or cartons that preserve freshness.
  • Compliance: Phytosanitary certificates and APEDA registration ensure regulatory alignment.

By focusing on these parameters, importers can minimize losses and ensure peak condition upon arrival.
