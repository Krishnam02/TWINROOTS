Key Quality Parameters Buyers Should Check When Importing Onions

A checklist for global onion buyers.

When importing onions, buyers must pay attention to international quality benchmarks to avoid supply chain issues. Key factors include:

  • Size & Shape: Consistency in bulb size for retail packaging.
  • Color & Skin: Fresh, uniform color without blemishes.
  • Firmness: Onions should be hard and free from soft spots.
  • Moisture Content: Ideal range to prevent sprouting or rotting.
  • Packing: Proper mesh bags or cartons to ensure ventilation during transport.

By checking these parameters, importers ensure long shelf life and customer satisfaction.
