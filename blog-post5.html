International Certifications That Matter for Onion Export

Compliance that builds trust in global markets.

Certifications are essential for smooth international onion exports. They assure buyers of quality, food safety, and compliance with global regulations.

  • APEDA: Export Development Authority recognition for agricultural exports.
  • HACCP: Hazard Analysis ensuring food safety compliance.
  • ISO Standards: Guaranteeing consistent quality processes.
  • GlobalG.A.P.: Certification for sustainable and safe farming practices.

These certifications act as trust badges for importers and ensure smoother trade relationships.
