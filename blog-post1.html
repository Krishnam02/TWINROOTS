Everything You Need to Know About Onions Before Importing from India

A buyer’s guide for sourcing Indian onions.

India is one of the largest exporters of onions worldwide. For importers, understanding sourcing practices, quality parameters, pricing, and logistics is critical to success. In this blog, we’ll cover everything you need to know before importing onions from India.

  • Diverse varieties available including red, white, and yellow onions.
  • Competitive pricing backed by large-scale production.
  • Seasonal availability and year-round exports from storage hubs.

By working with certified exporters, you ensure your onions meet global quality standards and reach your market reliably.
